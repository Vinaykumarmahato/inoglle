Internships at Inoglle

Internships offer students a fantastic opportunity to gain valuable work experience in a professional setting. They allow you to:

  • Apply your knowledge in a real-world environment: Put classroom learning to the test by working on real projects alongside experienced professionals.
  • Develop essential skills: Hone your communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and time management skills, all highly sought after by employers.
  • Explore potential career paths: Get a taste of different fields within the industry and discover your career interests.
  • Build your network: Connect with industry professionals who can provide valuable mentorship and future job opportunities.
  • Enhance your resume: Internship experience demonstrates initiative and sets you apart from other job candidates.

Become a Campus Ambassador

We're excited to offer the Inoglle Campus Ambassador Program (CAP) – a unique opportunity for students to gain valuable experience while promoting our virtual internship program to their peers.

What is the Inoglle CAP?

The CAP is an amazing way for students to:

  • Gain Experience: Develop skills in communication, leadership, and marketing by promoting Inoglle's internship program to your fellow students.
  • Earn Money: For each student you successfully recruit using your unique code, you'll earn 10 rupees, helping you cover expenses.
  • Boost Your Resume: Demonstrate initiative, marketing skills, and leadership qualities - all highly sought-after by employers.
  • Network & Build Relationships: Connect with Inoglle staff and fellow ambassadors, expanding your network and opening doors to future opportunities.
  • Free Virtual Internship: Participate in the Inoglle Virtual Internship Program at no cost.
Why Apply to be a Campus Ambassador?

Here's why you should consider joining the CAP:

  • Gain valuable experience: Develop transferable skills that will benefit you throughout your career.
  • Earn money to support your internship journey.
  • Build a strong network within the industry.
  • Enhance your resume and stand out to future employers.
  • Gain access to a free virtual internship program.
